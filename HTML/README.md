# HTML cheatsheet

### Characters

& nbsp; – space (non-breaking)  
& quot; - add quotation mark (“)  
& lt; - less than symbol (<)  
& gt; - greater than symbol (>)  
& amp; - the ‘&’ or ampersand symbol  
& copy; - copyright symbol  
& trade; - trademark symbol
